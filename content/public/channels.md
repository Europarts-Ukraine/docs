# Channels

Channels represent external sales destinations such as marketplaces, B2B portals, websites, or partner feeds.

A channel defines which products belong to a destination and which content, locales, and price types should be used there.

## Common Tasks

- review channel settings;
- understand channel scope rules;
- synchronize channel products;
- prepare channel-specific content;
- mark product content as ready;
- understand why a product is not available for export.

## Channel Readiness

Only products that satisfy channel scope and readiness rules should be exported.

Typical reasons a product is not ready:

- missing translation;
- missing price;
- missing required field;
- archived channel enrichment;
- product no longer matches channel scope;
- user has not approved the channel content.
