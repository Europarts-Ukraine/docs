# History

History shows changes to important records in BAS Platform.

It helps answer:

- who changed a record;
- when it changed;
- what fields changed;
- whether a record was deleted or restored;
- which operation produced the change.

## Common Tasks

- open record history;
- inspect global history;
- compare old and new values;
- verify whether an import or user changed a record;
- audit critical changes.

## Notes

History availability can depend on module permissions and retention settings.
