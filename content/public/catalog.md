# Catalog

The catalog is the central place for product-related data.

It includes products, categories, brands, attributes, product families, product models, warehouses, and stock records. The exact available fields can differ by installation because BAS Platform supports custom fields and custom entities.

## Common Tasks

- find a product;
- review product details;
- edit product fields when allowed;
- inspect category and brand data;
- check stock values;
- review product history;
- understand why a product appears in a channel or export.

## Important Concepts

- **Product** is a record managed by the platform.
- **Category** groups products into a tree or classification structure.
- **Brand** identifies the product brand or source-specific brand.
- **Attribute** stores structured product characteristics.
- **Stock** stores quantity by warehouse.
- **History** shows who changed a record and when.

## Troubleshooting

If a product is missing or outdated, check:

1. whether the latest import finished successfully;
2. whether the product was filtered by source or category;
3. whether it was soft-deleted or archived;
4. whether your role has access to the required module.
