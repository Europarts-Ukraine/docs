# FAQ

## Is PAD Platform an ERP?

No. PAD Platform complements ERP, CRM, and accounting systems. It focuses on product, supplier, pricing, content, channel, and export data.

## Can users access the source code?

No. Customer users work through the application interface and documented integrations.

## Why does the platform validate imports before writing data?

Validation protects the working catalog from partial or incorrect updates.

## Why is a product missing from an export?

Typical reasons include channel scope, missing required fields, missing prices, missing translations, or unresolved readiness issues.

## Can every field be edited manually?

No. Some fields are calculated, imported, read-only, or controlled by permissions.
